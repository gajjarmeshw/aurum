Setup Instructions
Step 1: Create Bot

1. Open Telegram and search @BotFather
2. Send /newbot
3. Follow prompts to get your BOT_TOKEN

Step 2: Get Chat ID

1. Message your new bot anything (or add it to a group)
2. Visit: api.telegram.org/bot<TOKEN>/getUpdates
3. Find chat.id in the JSON response
💡 Pro Tip: You can add multiple IDs separated by commas (e.g., ID1,ID2) to send alerts to multiple groups/users at once. Group IDs always start with a -.

Step 3: Configure .env
TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN=123456789:ABCdefGHI...
TELEGRAM_CHAT_ID=987654321
